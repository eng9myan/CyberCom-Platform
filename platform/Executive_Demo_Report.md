## 2. Integrated Platform Proof Points

### Hospital & Clinic EMR Operations
Seeded patient journeys validate outpatient consults, primary care appointments, and inpatient bed reservations:
- Emergency admissions, ward check-ins, and standard room bed allocations are operational.

### LIS & Radiology Integrations
- Lab sample collections, accession workflows, biochemistry results, and low/high interpretation flags function.
- body-part specific DICOM protocols, radiological studies, and radiology report impressions with built-in AI assistant summaries are supported.

### Pharmacy & Drug Safety
- Formulary drug listings (generic vs brand), therapeutic class codes, prescriptions, and pharmacy dispensations are linked.

### Revenue Cycle Management (RCM)
- Patient accounts link to corporate insurance plans (e.g. Bupa Arabia).
- Patient co-pay and insurance claim portions are calculated, claims submitted/approved, and payments logged.

### CyCom ERP Operations
- **Finance & Accounting:** Core Chart of Accounts (Cash, AR, Inventory, Fixed Assets, Payables, Revenue, Expense) is defined. Transactions (like Aisha's outpatient invoice) trigger general ledger **posted journal entries** balancing debit/credit lines.
- **HR & Payroll:** Staff are managed as employees in the clinical operations department. Payroll runs generate payslips for physicians and nurses.
- **Inventory & Assets:** Pharmacy stock items receive purchase order receipts. Fixed assets (like CT Scanners) trace purchase costs, salvage values, and depreciations.
- **BI Reports:** BI dashboard metrics monitor ER wait times and monthly gross revenues.
