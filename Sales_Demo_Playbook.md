## 1. Overview

This playbook outlines step-by-step interactive scripts for sales engineers to showcase CyberCom's interconnected healthcare and ERP solutions during prospect meetings.

---

## 2. Interactive Scripts & Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Outpatient Primary Care Journey
1. **Patient Registration:** Navigate to the Patient Portal. Show patient `Aisha Al-Otaibi` logging in and verifying her profile.
2. **Appointment Scheduling:** Schedule a primary care appointment with `Dr. Ahmad Hameed` at `Primary Care Clinic`.
3. **Clinic Consultation:** Log into the Outpatient Clinic Portal. Open Aisha's encounter. Document chief complaint (fatigue and abdominal discomfort) and order a **Vitamin D Lab Test** and **Abdominal Ultrasound**.
4. **Lab Processing:** Open the Laboratory Portal. Accession Aisha's blood sample, process it, and output a low result value (`12.5 ng/mL`).
5. **Imaging Analysis:** Open the Imaging Portal. Process the ultrasound order. Radiologist `Dr. Tareq Saleh` accesses the study and generates a report detailing "Grade 1 Hepatic Steatosis". Show the AI-assisted diagnostic summary generated.
6. **Medication Prescription:** Back in the Clinic Portal, review the lab and ultrasound results. Prescribe "One-Alpha Vitamin D3".
7. **Pharmacy Dispensing:** Open the Pharmacy Portal. Pharmacist `Dr. Rania Badawi` retrieves the prescription and logs the dispensation.

### Scenario 2: Emergency Department & Admission
1. **Emergency Admission:** Navigate to the Hospital EMR Portal. Create an emergency triage encounter for `Faisal Al-Shammari` presenting with acute chest pain.
2. **Clinical AI Assist:** Calculate Faisal's MELD/SOFA risk scores.
3. **Inpatient Ward Allocation:** Admit Faisal as an inpatient. Allocate him to a standard room bed (`MED-BED-2`) in the Medical Ward.
4. **Discharge & Billing:** Complete Faisal's inpatient stay, process discharge, and review billing invoices.
